About Sifra AI

Sifra AI is an AI-powered autonomous data scientist designed to simplify data analysis through NLP-driven automation. It converts natural language queries into executable code, supports Python, R, and SQL, and provides cloud-based storage for efficient collaboration. The platform enhances productivity with automated predictive analytics, intelligent data visualization, and real-time workflow execution.
